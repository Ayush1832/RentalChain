# 📘 PLATFORM DESIGN DOCUMENT

## Blockchain-Based Rental Evidence & Trust Infrastructure (India)

---

# 1. Executive Summary

India’s rental market is largely **informal, fragmented, and trust-deficient**, with limited standardisation in agreements, payments, and dispute handling. Most rental transactions lack verifiable audit trails, leading to frequent disputes, legal friction, and inefficiencies.

Unlike existing platforms that focus only on property discovery, this platform focuses on the **entire rental lifecycle** — from agreement creation to move-out — by introducing a **blockchain-based evidence and trust layer**.

The platform anchors key rental events such as:

* Agreements
* Payment records
* Property condition evidence  

on a **public blockchain**, ensuring tamper-proof, time-stamped, and verifiable records.

**Core Principle:**
Do not change how renting works — **digitise and secure it.**

# 3. Core Problems in Indian Rental Market

## 3.1 Tenant Problems

* Lack of reliable documentation for agreements and payments
* Disputes due to unclear or altered terms
* No verifiable rent history for loans/visa/employment
* No structured move-in/move-out evidence
* Informal communication leading to conflicts

## 3.2 Landlord Problems

* Lack of reliable tenant verification
* Payment tracking inconsistencies
* Property damage disputes
* Legal documentation complexity
* No standardised system for managing rentals

---

# 4. Platform Philosophy

## What the Platform Does NOT Do

* Does NOT replace legal rental agreements
* Does NOT hold user funds or deposits
* Does NOT introduce crypto or tokens
* Does NOT disrupt existing rental flow

## What the Platform DOES Do

* Anchors rental events on blockchain
* Creates tamper-proof evidence trails
* Standardises rental lifecycle processes
* Enables trusted verification across stakeholders
